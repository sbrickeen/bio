Blackpink

About

Blackpink is a girl group formed in South Korea consisting of four members: Jisoo, Jennie, Lisa, and Rosé.

They are the highest charting girl Kpop group ever.

Their single "Ddu-Du Ddu-Du" is the most viewed Korean music video on Youtube in a 24 hour period.

They began their careers in 2016.

Their albums consist of Korean and Japanese vocals.

Blackpink currently has 8 albums

Currently, they do not tour here in the United States.

Recently, they collabed with Dua Lipa to create the song "Kiss and Make Up" placing number 93 on America's Main Single chart.
